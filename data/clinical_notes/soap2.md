## Clinical Note

**Patient:** 75-year-old female  
**Chief Concern:** Concern for possible stroke

### Subjective
The patient is a 75-year-old female presenting with concerns about a possible stroke. She reports a sensation of something being stuck in her throat. This morning she experienced dizziness along with numbness in her left hand and left jaw. She notes that approximately three weeks ago she had a similar episode of dizziness that resulted in a fall. She was evaluated at that time by Dr. XYZ and was prescribed meclizine (Antivert).

Today she reports awakening during the night with numbness in her left hand and numbness involving the left side of her face and neck. She also reports having had an earache one to two days ago. She denies any recent cold symptoms.

### Plan
